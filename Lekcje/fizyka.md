## **Praca i moc prądu elektrycznego**
> ## `W = U*I*t` - praca
> `W = [V*A*s] = [V*(C/s)*s] = [(J/C)*C] = [J]`  
> `P = W/t = (U*I*t)/t`  
> `P = I*U`  
> `U = I*R`  
> `W = I^2*R*t`  
> `P = I^2*R`
